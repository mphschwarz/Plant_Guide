\section{Christmas cactus}

\subsection{watering}
\begin{itemize}
\item	Water from below.
\item	Be careful to not overwater it, it is still a cactus.
\item	Watering twice a month should be enough.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{pruning}
Prune after bloom in winter to not disurb the bloom.

\subsection{soil}
Use equal parts:
\begin{itemize}
\item	germination soil
\item	Perlite
\item	potting charcoal
\end{itemize}
